Spreadsheet Formulas Enterprise

The Total column is allowFormula: each cell holds a formula (=qty*price), and the last row totals the column with =SUM(D1:D5). Double-click a Qty or Unit Price cell and edit it: the row total and the grand total recalculate automatically. Double-click a Total cell to see or change its formula (type a leading =); references are highlighted and errors like #DIV/0! show inline. The computed value stays the cell value, so sorting and aggregation keep working.
